Welcome to our page!

Our page was created to give everyone a nice and easy way to log your travels and keep track of your journey around the world!!

On our page you will find called "Travel Logger" you will be able to enter, delete and search the countries you have travelled to, our page will also greet you with a counter after the first country has been entered leting you know how many places you have visited. When you enter the country you have visited you are now allowed to search (non case sensitive) for the country you have entered and it will display on the top of the page the details of your travels.
